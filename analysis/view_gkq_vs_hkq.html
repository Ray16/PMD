GKQ vs HKQ: The Q212 Bridge Paradox

The GKQ Paradox: Both GKQ and HKQ carry R147K + M212Q, but Ki differs 7-fold (GKQ=11 vs HKQ=80 mM). In HKQ (right), Q212 forms an H-bond bridge: Q212.OE1 — Y19.OH (2.72 Å) and Q212.OE1 — K22.NZ (2.79 Å), tethering loop 17-33 to the H4 helix and blocking the entrance. In GKQ (left), this bridge is absent despite the identical M212Q mutation. Additionally, K147 in HKQ forms a salt bridge with E144, stabilizing helix H2.
