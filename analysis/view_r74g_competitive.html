R74G: Competitive Active-Site Binding

R74G: Competitive binding mechanism. Without R74's guanidinium, L2 enters the active site itself (red), displacing L1 (green). This model has 10 contacts with catalytic residues. This is a different mechanism from the entrance binding seen in WT — consistent with R74G's extremely high Ki (110 mM, weak inhibition).
